NEED HELP GETTING STARTED?
This article will explain what you need to know to start working with Learnest
You will have to create a new account on LEARNEST to start uploading and downloading course content. Here are the steps:
1. Fill out the Registration form with your details OR login using Facebook or Google.
2. A validation mail will be send to the registered email address.
Click on the validation link.
3. Your account is now created and you can now log in.
Once you have successfully logged in:
1. How to subscribe?
Locate or search for the teachers, students or classes that you want to subscribe.
On the student/teacher profile page click on subsribe button.
You will get news on any updates of the subscribed teacher, student or class in your newsfeed.
2. How to upload a file?
Go to your profile page by clicking on profile link in navigation bar.
Fill in details about the file like Title, Description, Subject etc and upload the file.
